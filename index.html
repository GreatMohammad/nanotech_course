The Physics of Nano

Surface Area Dominance

As size decreases, the percentage of atoms on the surface increases drastically. This makes nanomaterials incredibly reactive (e.g., gold becomes a catalyst).

"If you divide a cube 1cm into 1nm cubes, the total surface area goes from 6 cm² to 6,000 m²!"

Quantum Effects

At this scale, the electronic properties are governed by quantum mechanics. Materials change color, conductivity, and magnetism (e.g., Quantum Dots).

"Electrons are restricted in motion, leading to discrete energy levels."

Fabrication Strategies

1. Top-Down Approach

Start with a bulk material and remove parts until you reach the desired shape. Think of a sculptor carving a statue.

  • • Photolithography (Microchips)
  • • Mechanical Milling
  • • Chemical Etching

2. Bottom-Up Approach

Building complex structures atom-by-atom or molecule-by-molecule. This is how nature builds life.

  • • Molecular Self-Assembly
  • • Chemical Vapor Deposition
  • • DNA Origami
